Following in Gromit’s footsteps, Shaun in the City saw 120 artist designed sculptures of Shaun the Sheep scattered across Bristol and London. Organised by the incredible team at The Grand Appeal, the trail was raising money for Bristol Children’s Hospital.

I was heavily involved in helping to develop the brand and roll it across a wide range of printed material and merchandise.

The indefatigable Matt Hitchcock was instrumental in the map department.
