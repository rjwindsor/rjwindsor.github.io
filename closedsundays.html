Closed Sundays work in retail design and build. Armed with a sharp logo type, they approached me to build a high end website and develop the brand in line with their distinguished client base.

True to their name, the website is closed on Sundays. In its place is a brief insight into Chris and Jon's time out of hours in their Sundays blog.

Illustration prowess was provided by one half of Forest and Fawn, the fantastically talented Emily Mullen, while digital wizardry was provided by Liam Richardson for the website build.
