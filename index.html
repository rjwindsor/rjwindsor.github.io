hello@richard-windsor.co.uk

I’m a designer based in Bristol with nearly a decade of experience in the creative industry, six of those spent as a senior designer at multi-disciplinary creative studio Beef.

Happiest when tinkering in digital or print, I’ve tackled; advertising, apps, artworking, branding, campaigns, communications, packaging, publications, typesetting, and website UI.

I’ve worked with clients from all walks of life including: Aardman, Alastair Sawday's, AXA, BDH, Good Energy, Kia, Nissan, Penguin and Puffin Books, the Royal Agricultural University, Severn Trent Water, Transparency International, and Studio Hardie.

If you’re an agency, company or individual with a design challenge lets talk about your project over snacks.
