The highly skilled team at Studio Hardie were up to their old tricks again, this time kitting out the interior of an all electric Nissan e-NV200 to go on a promotional tour of Europe. They asked me to help them complete the theme with a full vehicle wrap.

Highlighting the vans ability to fulfil a wide range of roles, I was inspired by sports court markings which overlap to allow a variety of games to be played within the same space.
