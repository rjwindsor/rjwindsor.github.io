Amazing Sheds was a collaboration between George Clarke and Studio Hardie. I was approached to design a logo and full page magazine advert in preparation for their launch at Grand Designs Live.

The logo type is developed from scratch and is designed to reflect the modular nature of the product. In the advert, working with a basic 3D model of the shed, I had to fully light, prop out, and set the shed in scene using Photoshop.
