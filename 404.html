hello@richard-windsor.co.uk

I’m a designer based in Bristol with over eight years experience in the creative industry, six of those spent as a senior designer at multi-disciplinary creative studio Beef.

Happiest when tinkering with the web or in print, I’ve tackled; advertising, apps, branding, campaigns, communications, publications, typesetting, and websites.

I’ve worked with clients from all walks of life including: Alastair Sawday's, AXA, BDH, Good Energy, Kia, Peguin and Puffin Books, the Royal Agricultural University, Severn Trent Water, Transparency International, and William Hardie.

If you’re an agency, company or individual with a design challenge lets talk about your project over snacks.
